# The School of Breath — AI Icon Generation System Guide

## Vision & Philosophy

The visual identity of The School of Breath should feel:

- Calm
- Premium
- Spiritual but modern
- Ancient wisdom translated into elegant technology
- Soft, healing, intelligent, sacred, and emotionally safe
- Minimal yet deeply meaningful

The icons should communicate:

- Breath
- Inner stillness
- Consciousness
- Nervous system regulation
- Energy flow
- Sacred geometry
- Sleep
- Meditation
- Transformation
- Healing through rhythm and awareness

The design language must avoid:

- Cheap wellness aesthetics
- Generic yoga clipart
- Cartoon spirituality
- Overly busy details
- Harsh contrast
- Neon cyberpunk styles
- Corporate tech feeling
- Religious symbolism that feels culturally narrow

The goal is to create a timeless spiritual-tech aesthetic similar to:

- Luxury wellness apps
- Calm nervous system interfaces
- Apple-level minimalism
- Sacred geometry mixed with modern UI
- Meditation + neuroscience + breathwork fusion
